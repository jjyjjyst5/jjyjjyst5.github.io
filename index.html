What she does

A receptionist who never sleeps.

When your office closes, Samantha answers. She handles the calls that would otherwise land in voicemail, and hands your staff a clean summary in the morning.

📅

Real appointments, real calendar

Samantha checks your practice's actual open slots and books directly into your EMR or scheduling system. She never invents availability and never double-books.

👋

Knows your patients

Returning patients are recognized by their verified caller ID and greeted by first name. Identity is confirmed with name and date of birth before any appointment is discussed or changed.

💬

Messages that reach you

Callback requests, questions for staff, and refill requests to pass along are captured accurately and relayed to your team - with message content kept in a secure console, never in the notification itself.

🚨

Emergency aware

Every greeting routes true emergencies to 911 up front, and emergency language is detected in code, before the AI ever responds. Samantha never triages and never schedules around an emergency.

🛡

Guardrails written in code

Booking caps, identity verification, rate limits, and data validation are enforced deterministically by software, not left to the judgment of a language model. The AI proposes; the code disposes.

🩺

Stays in her lane

Samantha schedules and relays. She never gives medical advice, never interprets symptoms or results, and never makes clinical or billing decisions. Anything clinical goes to your staff.

“Hi, this is Samantha. It's after hours here at the office, but I can get you on the schedule, or take a message for the staff. How can I help you?”

Warm, calm, and professional - the voice you're glad to reach when you're worried.

Safety & trust

Built for medicine, not adapted to it.

Healthcare calls carry protected health information, and Samantha is engineered around that reality from the first line of code.

✓

HIPAA-minded architecture. Protected health information is processed only on HIPAA-eligible, BAA-covered infrastructure, with a compliance posture that is verified in code at startup - a non-compliant configuration refuses to run.

✓

Zero-PHI notifications. Staff notifications say only that a message exists. The content stays behind an authenticated console, so no patient information rides in a text message.

✓

Identity before information. No appointment details are shared or changed until the caller confirms their full name and date of birth. Known callers are greeted by first name only.

✓

Redacted logs, bounded retention. System logs never reproduce caller speech or full phone numbers. Records are retained only as long as the practice requires, with audit trails kept per HIPAA documentation rules.

✓

Honest by design. Samantha never claims an appointment is booked or cancelled unless the scheduling system confirmed it. When she is unsure, she says so and takes a message.
