SMS Messaging Programs

Effective July 19, 2026

Samantha Virtual Front Desk operates two SMS messaging programs on a single number for medical practices she serves. Both programs are described below, including how to opt in, what messages you will receive, and how to stop at any time.

Program 1: Patient Messaging

What it is. Two-way text conversation between a patient and Samantha, the practice's virtual front desk. Samantha can collect a message or appointment request and relay it to the practice's staff. No medical advice is given over SMS.

How to opt in. Text the practice's published Samantha number. The act of sending a text message to the number is your opt-in to that conversation. Samantha's first reply includes the following disclosure:

You're texting Samantha, the virtual front desk. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to end this conversation. Privacy: samantha-medical.com/privacy-policy / Terms: samantha-medical.com/terms-and-conditions

Message frequency. Varies by conversation. You will only receive messages in direct response to texts you send.

Program 2: Practice Staff Notifications

What it is. One-way SMS alerts sent to opted-in practice staff when Samantha takes a new patient message or completes a notable action (for example, booking or cancelling an appointment). Staff alerts never contain patient names, message content, or protected health information (PHI).

How to opt in. Staff members opt in directly with their practice through a verbal or written request (not a public web sign-up). After opting in, staff receive the following confirmation message:

Thank you for opting in to Samantha Practice Notifications. Msg frequency varies (0-10/day). Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out.

Message frequency. Varies based on call volume; typically 0-10 messages per day.

Opt-Out (STOP)

Reply STOP to any message from Samantha to opt out. You will receive a final confirmation:

You have successfully opted out. You'll receive no further messages. Reply START to resume. Msg & data rates may apply.

Help (HELP)

Reply HELP to any message from Samantha for support information. You will receive:

Thank you for contacting Samantha. Email info@samantha-medical.com for support. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg & data rates may apply.

Rates and Carriers

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Samantha's SMS programs are available on all major U.S. carriers.

No Shared Data

No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details on how information is handled.

Contact

For questions about these SMS programs, contact info@samantha-medical.com.
